#### Common Formats

argument | output
---------|--------
```l \t\h\e jS``` | Wednesday the 15th
```jS M Y``` | 15th March 2014
```l jS \of F Y h:i:s A``` | Monday 8th of August 2005 03:12:46 PM
```Y-m-d``` | 2010-02-06



#### For interacting with other systems

argument | output
---------|--------
```Y-m-d H:i:s``` | 2010-02-06 19:30:13 - good for MySQL
